Hello! My name is Domas and I'm an enthusiast full-stack developer, based in Lithuania. I love building web applications and learning new technologies. My main focus is on Python (And a grasp of Java), but I also enjoy working with Dart as a front-end language. When I'm not coding, you can find me reading tech blogs, folding origami, riding bikes with friends or family, or experimenting with new ideas in maths and physics fields. What I'm learning currently is AI technologies, especially in the context of agentic applications. Perfect example of this is one of my projects - EchoTuner, which is an AI-powered Spotify playlist creation app.

The longer I live, the more I realize the capability and advantages of constant learning. I'm not afraid to make mistakes, but I'm always striving to improve and reach perfectionism. I believe that with hard work and dedication, anything is possible. Feel free to reach out if you want to collaborate on a project or just chat about tech!
